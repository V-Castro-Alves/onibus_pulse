## 🗺️ Project Roadmap

Welcome to the Onibus Pulse roadmap! This document outlines our planned features and improvements. We're excited to make the app even better and welcome community feedback.

---

### 🎯 Near-Term Goals (Up Next)

*These are our highest-priority features currently in planning or active development.*

- [ ] **⭐ Favorites & Offline Caching**
  - *Description:* Save your most-used routes and stops for one-tap access. Key data will be cached to work even when you're offline or have a poor connection.

- [ ] **🔭 Multi-Route Watch Mode**
  - *Description:* Create a custom dashboard to monitor live ETAs from several different routes and stops on a single screen. Perfect for commuters with transfer points.

### 🚀 Mid-Term Goals (Coming Soon)

*Features we're planning to tackle after completing our near-term goals.*

- [ ] **📍 Map Geofencing & Trip Alerts**
  - *Description:* Get a notification when your bus is approaching your stop or when you're nearing your destination. No more staring at the map!

- [ ] **📊 Crowdsourced Accuracy Feedback**
  - *Description:* A simple way for users to report if an ETA was accurate. This data will help us fine-tune our ETA engine over time.

- [ ] **📉 Low-Data "Text" Mode**
  - *Description:* An ultra-lightweight interface that provides essential ETA information with minimal data usage, ideal for slow networks or tight data plans.
